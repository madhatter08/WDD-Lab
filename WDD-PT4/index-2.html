Barangay Makiling

The Story Behind Makiling

Barangay Makiling is the farthest Barangay of Calamba towards Batangas. The length of the barangay goes from the Island Gas stand on the national highway to the big bridge with the symbol of the balisong and horse in Sto.Tomas Batangas.

When the forest was cleared and farmers could see healthy crops, this place was called San Isidro. This is because San Isidro is the patron saint of farmers. The early inhabitants of San Isidro united their own to be the patron saint.

But later on, the name Makiling gradually prevailed among the people passing through it because of the many slopes of the Maria Makiling mountain.

Vision

Aspiration of Barangay Makiling to be

1. Prosperous and progressive that will help raise the level of living of his people
2. Asensado is capable of providing fast and complete basic services to all sectors of his community.
3. Helper of citizens in the development of their life.
4. Instrument in being God-loving, patriotic and environmentally friendly for its residents.
5. Open and honest in the leadership and implementation of projects and its programs.
6. A Barangay that is peaceful and pleasant for the people to live here.
7. A united community that participates in every project and program of the Government.
8. Government that will serve responsibly to its citizens.

Mission

Based on the 8-Point Vision of Barangay Makiling

Have free communication, cooperation and unity among the leaders of the Barangay and the people who are under it to quickly and properly realize the 8 Point Vision of our Barangay.

Core Values

Integrity

Barangay Makiling upholds integrity by promoting transparent and accountable governance practices. Its leaders prioritize honesty and ethical conduct, ensuring that community decisions and actions are guided by moral principles to foster trust among residents.

Excellence

Barangay Makiling demonstrates excellence by delivering high-quality public services to its residents. Whether in health care, education, or infrastructure, we strive for continuous improvement, setting a standard for outstanding performance within the community.

Leadership

The leaders of Barangay Makiling exhibit strong leadership by actively engaging with the community, listening to concerns, and providing clear direction. We inspire residents to participate in local initiatives, fostering a sense of unity and shared responsibility for the barangay's well-being.

Results

Barangay Makiling achieves tangible results by implementing effective programs that directly benefit the community. Through strategic planning and collaboration, we ensure that initiatives lead to positive outcomes and an overall higher quality of life for its residents.

Barangay Issuance

Barangay Makiling extends a range of essential services to its residents and businesses, including issuing Barangay Clearances and Business Clearances, providing Certificates of Indigency, and furnishing Certificates of Residency.

Barangay Clearance

Confirming that the individual has complied with community requirements and has no outstanding obligations or legal issues within the barangay.
